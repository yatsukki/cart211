WHAT IS SURVEILLANCE
CAPITALISM

interview  by: joanna kavenna
Zuboff’s work describes how global tech companies such as Google and Facebook persuaded us to give up our privacy for the sake of convenience; how  personal information (“data”) gathered by these companies has been used  by others not only to predict our behaviour but also to influence and  modify it; and how this has had disastrous consequences for democracy  and freedom. This is the “surveillance capitalism” of the title, which  Zuboff defines as a “new economic order” and “an expropriation of  critical human rights that is best understood as a coup from above”.
